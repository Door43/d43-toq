====== 1. Nyesubye ka Ŋibworekeya  ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-01.jpg?direct&}}

Nyepitye lo esyakinea nyibwore daani̱ lo: Asubi Nyakuju̱ nyakopiti̱ tya nyibwore daani̱ ni ayayi tooma na kopiti̱ ka ŋirwa ka ŋikani̱-kapei. Kerumwo Nyakuju̱ nyakusubu̱ nyakopo̱, ekamusikiniti̱, toworori wuu, amamu̱ nyibwore asubunitae. Tarai ecaadae Nyipari̱ ka Nyakuju̱ kalo kidyaama ŋakipi.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-02.jpg?direct&}}

Tarai abu Nyakuju̱ tolimu̱ teme̱, “Nyakica bu!” Tatakanu jiki̱ nyakica. Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyakica, toryamu̱ ejoko̱, tolimwoki̱ nayi nyekiro bee “Nyapaarani̱.” Totyaka̱ iŋesi̱ nyapaarani̱ ka nyakamusi̱, tolimwoki̱ ŋina nyekiro bee “Nyakwaari̱.” Asubuni Nyakuju̱ nyakica nyapaarani̱ nakalo kiŋareni̱ na esyakinea iŋesi̱ nyesubye ŋibworekeya.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg?direct&}}

Nyapaarani̱ naka ŋaarei, kurworiki̱ Nyakuju̱, tosubu̱ nyapaka̱ nakalo kidyaama nyakopo̱. Tosubwaki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyapaka̱ ŋina, totyakea ŋakipi nakalo kidyaama kana kalo kopo̱.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg?direct&}}

Nyapaarani̱ naka ŋawuni, kurworiki̱ Nyakuju̱, totyaka ŋakipi ka nyakopo̱ ni ewoniti̱. Tolimwoki̱ iŋesi̱ ni ewoniti̱ nyekiro bee “Nyakopiti̱,” tolimwokinite ŋakipi bee “Ŋanamea.” Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyatemari̱ ejoko̱ nyibwore ni asubuni iŋesi̱.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg?direct&}}

Tolimu̱ nabo Nyakuju̱ teme̱, “Too nyakopo̱ toruputu̱ ŋinyia ka ŋatekerini̱ tya ŋikito lugelagela.” Apotu̱ ŋuna tosubwakini̱. Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyatemari̱ ejoko̱ nyibwore ni asubuni iŋesi̱.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-06.jpg?direct&}}

Nyapaarani̱ naka ŋooŋwono̱, kurworiki̱ Nyakuju̱, tosubu̱ nyakoloŋo̱, nyelapa̱ tya ŋakacere̱ ka ŋakanyere̱. Asubuni Nyakuju̱ ikesi̱ tocayikisi̱ nyakopo̱, nabo kitemere nyapaarani̱ ka nyakwaari̱, tya ŋatyaketa ka nyekaru ka ŋikaru̱. Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyatemari̱ ejoko̱ nyibwore ni asubuni iŋesi̱.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-07.jpg?direct&}}

Nyapaarani̱ naka ŋakani̱, kurworiki̱ Nyakuju̱, tosubu̱ ŋibworekeya daani̱ lu elomito tooma na kipi tya ŋikyenyi̱ daani̱. Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyatemari̱ ejoko̱ nyibwore ni asubuni iŋesi̱. Abu iŋesi̱ togeata̱ ikesi̱.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg?direct&}}

Nyapaarani̱ naka ŋakani̱-kapei, tolimu̱ Nyakuju̱ teme̱, “Too ŋityaŋe̱ pena na kopo̱ ka ŋatekerini̱ kece̱ egelagela.” Tosubwakini̱ ŋuna logeri̱ lo alimuno Nyakuju̱. Ŋityaŋe̱ ŋulu arai ŋicye lu palemu̱ ebariitae, ŋicye arai lu kalyaka̱ kalo kopo̱, tarai lu cye ŋityaŋe̱ luka nyamoni. Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyatemari̱ ejokaka̱.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg?direct&}}

Tolimu̱ nayi Nyakuju̱ teme̱: “Toosiki̱ atosubwakisi ŋituŋa, ikwaana̱ ka iŋwoni̱, topolokisi̱ nyakopo̱ tya ŋityaŋe̱ daani̱.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-10.jpg?direct&}}

Tolimu̱ nayi Nyakuju̱ teme̱: “Toosiki̱ atosubwakisi ŋituŋa, ikwaana̱ ka iŋwoni̱, topolokisi̱ nyakopo̱ tya ŋityaŋe̱ daani̱.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg?direct&}}

Abu Nyakuju̱ kita nyamana̱ kuwaaki̱ ŋikito ŋeaare lumunono; nyekitoe loka nyakiyara̱, lo cye arai nyekitoe lo yenunete̱ naka jokaka̱ ka naka ronoko̱. Tolimwoki̱ Nyakuju̱ Adamo nyatemari̱ icuri iŋesi̱ tonyama̱ ŋarayeta ka ŋikito ka daani̱ kalo mana, tarai nyenyama lo yenunete̱ naka jokaka̱ tya naka ronoko̱. Ani erai kenyama ŋarayeta ka nyekitoe ka ŋolo, etwakete ikesi̱.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg?direct&}}

Kaneni tolimu̱ Nyakuju̱ teme̱, “Esilikiniti̱ nyekilye erai iŋesi̱ aboni̱.” Tarai amamu̱ kalo tooma ŋityaŋe̱ nyepei daŋa̱ lo icuri tarau nyekeŋarakinani̱ Adamo.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg?direct&}}

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-14.jpg?direct&}}

Kaneni tolimu̱ Nyakuju̱ teme̱, “Esilikiniti̱ nyekilye erai iŋesi̱ aboni̱.” Tarai amamu̱ kalo tooma ŋityaŋe̱ nyepei daŋa̱ lo icuri nyekeŋarakinani̱ Adamo.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg?direct&}}

Tosubu̱ nayi Nyakuju̱ nyekilye ka nyaberu, elemarito ŋipityesyo keŋe̱. Togeata̱ nayi Nyakuju̱ ikesi̱ tolimu̱ teme̱, “Towureata ŋide, towuruutu̱ lu kusi̱ ŋitatae, kilelebunwosi̱ nyakopo̱.” Kiyokoki̱ Nyakuju̱ ŋibworekeya lu asubuni iŋesi̱ daani̱, taanyu̱ ejoko̱. Talakari̱ iŋesi̱ loowoi kotere nyibwore ŋini daani̱ ni asubuni iŋesi̱. Na asubwakinosi nyapaarani̱ naka ŋakani̱-kapei ikesi̱ ŋuna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-16.jpg?direct&}}

Na anaŋea nyapaarani̱ naka ŋakani̱-kaare, kirikaki̱ Nyakuju̱ nyetici̱ keŋe̱ daani̱. Kiyeŋa nayi Nyakuju̱ kalo tici̱ kalo asubi iŋesi̱ daani̱.

Kimara̱ nyeemutu̱ lo kanika: Nyakisyawunete̱ 1-2.
